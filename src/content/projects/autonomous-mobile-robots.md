## Overview

ABU Robocon 2024 asked for robots that could carry out a sequence of manipulation tasks entirely on their own. Leading a 32-person team, I drove the design and integration of two four-wheel holonomic robots that navigate, pick, kick, and sort — no human in the loop.

## What I built

- **Encoder-based navigation** for repeatable waypoint following.
- **Computer vision** for colour-based object detection and sorting.
- **Custom control algorithms** binding perception to actuation across the task sequence.

## Highlights

The project combined mechanical design in SolidWorks, custom PCB development in Altium, and in-house manufacturing — CNC machining, laser cutting, additive manufacturing, and PCB fabrication — to realise a genuinely end-to-end autonomous system. We qualified for the national round with a Robot Design Documentation score of **95/100**.
