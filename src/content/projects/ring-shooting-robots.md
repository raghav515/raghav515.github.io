## Overview

My first full Robocon build. For ABU Robocon 2023, our 12-person team developed two three-wheel holonomic robots that navigate omnidirectionally, collect rings, and shoot them accurately at several target heights.

## What I built

- A **spring-loaded catapult** and a **dual-flywheel launcher** with a **custom gearbox** for variable-height shooting.
- Two **custom PCBs** for power distribution and hardware integration.
- A **NodeMCU-based wireless control system** running custom navigation algorithms.

## Highlights

Working across Fusion 360, PCB design, CNC milling, laser cutting, polymer 3D printing, TIG welding, soldering, and mechanical assembly, we delivered a competition-ready platform and qualified for the national round — scoring **96/100** in Robot Design Documentation, our highest yet.
